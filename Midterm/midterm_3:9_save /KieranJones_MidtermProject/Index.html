Our Specialized Services

In addition to standard locksmith services, we offer:

High-Security Master Keying

From small to large scales, we create master key setups for both BiLock and Abloy Protec product lines. Small setups start at $15 per cylinder.

Key Duplication

In addition to most non-high-security keys, we offer duplication services of Abloy Protec, Abloy Classic, BiLock, ASSA Twin, and Medeco M3/Biaxial/Classic keys.

Collector Locks

We have cutaway locks that show the internals of the lock, rare locks, and large lot locks for high security locks at bargain rates.
